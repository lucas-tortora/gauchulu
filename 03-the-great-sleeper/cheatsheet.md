## CHAPTER 7 — LA VÍSPERA (1 PAGE)

**GOAL**: Last conversations → confessions → Mercedes arrives → entity speaks

**SCENE FLOW**

1. **The Pit at Night, Changed**

* The pit is deeper than it was in C1; the carved wall extends two meters further down
* Eight panels at the outer ring (Quirce's, marked); the asymmetric one *can still be fixed*
* Continuous low vibration, uneven (3c)

2. **Don Eusebio's Confession**

* About Concepción: *"There were nine more weeks of that reasoning."*
* He rode east not to protect her but *to avoid watching her either die or save everyone*
* Honesty is the redemption; the action was cowardice
* If the players saved her in C1: he makes eye contact with that PC

3. **Nahuel — What He Knows**

* The entity *learns* the geometry as the ritual deploys it
* He has three weeks of focus-work behind him; he is the default anchor
* He has made his peace. He will not perform calm — he sustains it.

4. **Kuyen — What She Won't Say**

* Mapuche counter-chant *Newen mapu kelluiñ* (named, finally) (4)
* The Wrong Returned are *anchors*, not victims. Necessary.
* She does not ask for help. Concrete offers get precise instructions.

5. **The Cursed Gaucho Arrives Uninvited**

* Tells his Coypac story. Names *Dadálah* again.
* He is here for El Patrón, not for the geometry.

6. **Mercedes Arrives, Late**

* Embraces Kuyen (thirty years of friendship visible for one breath)
* Names the Necronomicón as the book Saens copied from
* Mercedes touches a player's cuff: she recognizes the dust (3b)
* Concepción is at the fire, humming a different tune (B8)

7. **The Entity Speaks**

* Through the wind, through the vibration, addressed to the Marked:
* *"Conmigo, sería diferente."* — *"With me, it would be different."*
* SAN 1/1D6

**KEY DIALOGUE**

* Don Eusebio: *"I couldn't bear to see whether you would save her or whether she would save you."*
* Mercedes: *"Es el Necronomicón. Saens lo copió."*

**ATMOSPHERIC RUNNERS**

* The Zonda (hot wind) at the pit; flies hovering motionless above the carving
* Concepción humming a tune that almost matches the vibration, then doesn't

**END**

* Dawn is in three hours. Ritual begins at first light. No one sleeps well.

**GM NOTES**

* This is a low-action chapter; intersperse confessions with practical preparations
* The entity's offer to each Marked PC is whispered, individual, *tempting*
* If Quirce's diagram error wasn't caught in C6: Silvio offers a safety-net Spot Hidden here

## CHAPTER 8 — LA TRAICIÓN (1 PAGE)

**GOAL**: Run the five-phase ritual → catch the betrayal → hold the geometry through pressure

**STRUCTURE: 5 PHASES**

| Phase | What it is | Pressure |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Outer ring placement; chant begins | Low |
| 2 | Inner ring panels activate; Wrong Returned anchor | Medium |
| 3 | Center focus (Nahuel); the geometry *recognizes* the PCs | High |
| 4 | Quirce's move — **the betrayal** | Highest |
| 5 | Final compression; the entity offers the deal | Sustained |

**PHASE 1–2 — ASSEMBLY**

* Quirce assigns positions; Kuyen overrides the asymmetric panel if caught earlier
* Saúl Carrasco arrives from the west with a hand-copied Coypac alignment (sets up C9 echo)

**PHASE 3 — DON EUSEBIO IN THE RING**

* The entity offers him *abundance, prosperity, renewal*
* He attempts the prayer *Por la tierra que fue buena* but cannot finish it (4)
* If Mercedes is present: she steps in for the anchor's eastern position

**PHASE 4 — QUIRCE'S MOVE (BETRAYAL)**

* He attempts to redirect the closing into a binding-for-use
* Counter: catch + correct in 1 round (Cthulhu Mythos / INT)
* If the asymmetric panel was caught in C6/C7: clean redirect
* If not: outer ring breaks; one Wrong Returned dies; SAN 1/1D6
* **Cursed Gaucho breaks formation when El Patrón is briefly visible at the western edge**

**PHASE 5 — THE OFFER**

* Mercedes's brief speech to the Marked PCs (D3): a shared-perception beat, not a curriculum
* Names the book and the city explicitly
* **READER-PATH FORESHADOW (2b)**: Mercedes addresses the PC who read Saens aloud — *"You read Saens out loud, didn't you. I felt it from the eastern ring."*
* Mapudungun counter-chant joined by all NPCs in the outer ring (4)

**MARK BURNS**

* Marked PCs roll 2d6 at key moments. Double 1: the Hound returns for 1d3 rounds.
* Use sparingly. 3 times max across the chapter.

**ATMOSPHERIC RUNNERS**

* Vibration peaks at Phase 5
* Dust still on the players — observable: it visibly *shifts* in resonance with the chant

**END**

* Phase 5 holds. Phase 6 (the closing) is C9.

**GM NOTES**

* Pre-chart anchor positions on a sheet of paper
* This is the densest chapter mechanically — give the players a visual aid
* Don't let the entity's offers be vague; tailor each to a specific PC's known want

## CHAPTER 9 — EL CIERRE (1 PAGE)

**GOAL**: Run the closing → name and pay the cost → set up the ride east

**ROUND 1 — THE FINAL CONTRACTION**

* Outer ring, inner ring, center hold
* Carved lines compress and go dark in sequence (deepest first)
* Anchors: Nahuel (default), or Marta + Héctor co-anchor (best survival), or a Marked PC

**ROUND 2 — NAHUEL'S DECISION**

* The entity offers him a final out
* If a co-anchor holds his hands: their presence *is* a vote for the closing

**ROUND 3 — THE SEAL**

* Kuyen draws the last line
* **The sound stops. Not fades — stops.** The vibration ends. (3c)
* Pit is once more a meter deep; the extra two meters are gone (not collapsed)
* SAN 1/1D6 — the shock of *over*
* **The dust on the players has not lifted.** Marked PC notices. Kuyen: *"Lo de acá se cerró. Lo de allá no."* (3b — sets up Arc 4)

**THE COST**

* **Best case** (all Marked alive, diagram correct, co-anchor): every PC pays a *named* cost — pre-identified per concept (a family member's name, a field of knowledge, a limb, faith, return-home)
* **Anchor dies**: Nahuel by default; Eusebio if he stood in for the eastern position; a PC if they volunteered
* **Catastrophic failure**: pit reopens, ~20km unlivable; Arc 4 still happens as rear-guard

**AFTERMATH SCENES**

* Rosa: dies of old age within the year; gives back the silver four-line ornament — to be returned to Mercedes
* Don Eusebio (if alive): asks *"What do I owe?"*
* Concepción: *"He was very afraid. But he stayed."* (her last Arc 3 line)
* Goat in San Ruiz well: a Marked PC sees she has six pupils per eye (3a)
* Rosa's silent prayer (4) — six words, between Rosa and the player who watches her say them

**THE COYPAC NEWS — DAWN**

* Saúl Carrasco arrives, breathless, with a hand-copied page from a Coypac cultist
* Names the **Manzana de las Luces vault** in Buenos Aires
* The book Saens copied from is *there*
* Mercedes: *"Tenemos que ir."*

**END**

* One quiet day at the estancia for breath, burials, one domestic scene per investigator
* Then mount up. Ride east. Four to six days.

**GM NOTES**

* Take the time. Each PC's cost named *aloud* by the Keeper.
* Don't pre-empt Arc 4's emotional landing — it comes at C12, not here.
* If the table chooses not to ride east: campaign ends here, Arc 3 only. Honor it.

## ARC 3 SNAPSHOT

* **Theme**: Closing one wound while a larger one opens elsewhere
* **Emotions**: Resolve → Cost → Recognition (the work is not done)
* **Seeds**: The book named, the vault named, the Reader recognized, Mercedes's confession promised, El Patrón briefly on stage, the Cursed Gaucho's hunt set
* **Rule**: The pit closes; the dust does not. The closing is *partial* by design — the campaign has not ended.
