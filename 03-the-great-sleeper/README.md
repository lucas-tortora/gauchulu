# ARC 3 — THE GREAT SLEEPER (Chapters 7–9)

Per `overview.md`, this arc moves the players toward the Andes, makes the **Cult of the Great Sleeper** the central threat, surfaces the **Cursed Gaucho's** backstory, and ends with the players accepting that the **Necronomicon** may be necessary.

The detailed prep currently in this folder reflects an **earlier campaign draft** in which Arc 3 is *El Cierre* — the closing ritual at the burial pit, structured as a five-phase ceremony. Quirce makes his betrayal move; Don Eusebio's southern position is the campaign's moral hinge; Marta and Héctor (the Wrong Returned), Silvio, Kuyen, and Nahuel pay the ritual's costs. Use the existing prep as the foundation; the *Expansion Notes* section appended to each chapter bridges that prep to the Great Sleeper / Andes framing.

## Arc Plot Points (overview.md)

- Journey toward the Andes and old cult routes.
- Cult of the Great Sleeper becomes central threat.
- Zonda winds caused by locust hive-mind around Salamanca influence.
- The Great Sleeper cult seeks awakening through ritual and the book.
- Cursed Gaucho reveals massacre of his family.
- Failed ritual encounter outside Salamanca tied to awakening attempt.
- Doña Mercedes explains the necessity of the Necronomicon.
- Players begin to believe the book may be necessary.
