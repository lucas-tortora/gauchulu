# ARC 1 — LA TIERRA MALA (ULTRA-CONDENSED GM SHEETS)

---

## SESSION 1 — THE CONTRACT → THE PIT (1 PAGE)

**GOAL**: Sell false premise → reveal pattern → first supernatural event

**SCENE FLOW**

1. **Estancia (Hire)**

* NPCs: Don Eusebio (blames tribes), Tomás (afraid)
* Give: tracks to tribal lands, branded hide with strange marks
* Hook: inconsistencies if pressed

2. **Cattle Field**

* Visual: bodies in circles, precise cuts, no feeding
* Clues: intentional placement, metallic smell
* SAN 0/1

3. **Tracks → Pit**

* Tracks loop/vanish → lead to depression

4. **Burial Pit**

* Layered carcasses, blood patterns
* Tension: wind stops, flies silent

5. **La Luz Mala**

* Behavior: circles, reacts to breath/movement
* Options: observe/flee (attacks ineffective)
* Effects: SAN 0/1D4; CON or nausea
* Reveal: traces same geometry

**END**

* Light departs → pit = full ritual circle
* Line: “This wasn’t done by animals.”

**GM NOTES**

* Keep it subtle; don’t confirm anything
* Seed: geometry matters

---

## SESSION 2 — SIGNS → THE CHAPEL (1 PAGE)

**GOAL**: Break assumptions → show counter-ritual → tragedy (La Llorona)

**SCENE FLOW**

1. **Tracking**

* Tracks inconsistent; footsteps out of sync

2. **San Ruiz (Empty Village)**

* NPC: Rosa (“They went… willingly.”)

3. **Chapel (Key)**

* Burned; two layers of symbols:

  * Carved (original)
  * Scratched (counter/binding)
* SAN 0/1

4. **La Llorona**

* Non-hostile, mournful; moves to key symbols
* Leads to hidden remains (child bones optional)
* Lines: “They didn’t listen…”, “You’re too late…”
* SAN 0/1D4 (1/1D6 if provoked)

**CLUE**

* Binding symbol fragment recovered

**END**

* She fades; ash briefly forms a circle

**GM NOTES**

* Emphasize: not all rituals are the same
* Build doubt before Session 3

---

## SESSION 3 — BROKEN RITUAL → HOUND (1 PAGE)

**GOAL**: Reveal truth → player-caused catastrophe → survival set-piece

**SCENE FLOW**

1. **Observe Encampment Ritual**

* NPCs: Kuyen (leader), Nahuel (unstable focus)
* Clue: symbols are reversed (binding)

2. **Decision**

* Likely interrupt → collapse
* Effects: wind surge, fire dies; SAN 0/1D4

3. **Massacre (Hound)**

* 4 tribe members die instantly
* SAN 1/1D6

4. **HOLD THE CIRCLE (3 ROUNDS)**

* Objective: protect Kuyen/Nahuel, survive 3 rounds
* Options: guard / break angles / assist ritual / attack (limited)
* R1 Chaos → R2 Pressure → R3 Climax (near breach)

**SUCCESS**

* Ritual completes; entity collapses into angles and vanishes

**AFTERMATH**

* Lines: “We were stopping it.” / “You helped it grow.”
* Truth: tribe resists; El Patrón responsible; rituals incomplete

**MARK (If hit & survive)**

* 2d6 at key moments; double 1 → entity returns (1d3 rounds)

**END**

* El Patrón seen watching from afar

**GM NOTES**

* Fast, sharp descriptions; no exposition mid-combat
* Consequences teach the lesson

---

## ARC 1 SNAPSHOT

* **Theme**: Act on bad info → consequences
* **Emotions**: Unease → Doubt → Guilt
* **Seeds**: Geometry, binding vs opening, incomplete knowledge
* **Rule**: Never explain—let outcomes prove it
