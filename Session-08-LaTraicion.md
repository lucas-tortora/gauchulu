# SESSION 8 — LA TRAICIÓN
### *The Betrayal*

**Session Goal**: The ritual begins. Quirce makes his final move. Don Eusebio faces the moment. The entity presses for breach. Establish what Session 9 will need to overcome.
**Emotional Arc**: Commitment → Crisis → Desperate Holding
**Key Seed**: The betrayal isn't just Quirce's. Don Eusebio has one more lie in him. Whether he tells it determines everything.

---

## OPENING SITUATION

Morning after the eve. The group prepares. Kuyen draws the final binding panel at dawn. Nahuel redraws his palm markings. Silvio, Héctor, and Marta take positions at the outer edge of the pit's boundary without being asked — they know where they need to stand.

The ritual is scheduled for dusk — the same hour Kuyen used for the partial bindings, when the light makes the geometry of shadows longest and the entity's ability to move through surface angles is slightly compromised. Six hours remain.

Six hours in which nothing should go wrong, and two things do.

---

## FIRST FRACTURE: DON EUSEBIO'S DEFECTION

Somewhere between noon and late afternoon, Don Eusebio disappears.

Not permanently. He does not flee. But an investigator who looks for him after the midday heat will find him: at the edge of the south pasture, standing at the fence line in the position Silvio used to occupy, gripping the topmost wire, looking south at the pit.

He is not in distress. He is considering.

**If not intercepted**: He will return on his own after an hour. He will be quieter and more resolved than before — in a way that could be resolution toward the ritual or resolution toward something else.

**If intercepted**: He will not lie about what he was thinking.

> *"I was thinking about leaving."* He says this plainly. *"Getting on a horse and riding north. Getting on a train. Not being here for whatever happens tonight."*

*(Pause.)*

> *"I didn't leave. I'm still here. But I wanted you to know I thought about it. So whatever I do tonight, you know I had the choice and I didn't take it. I want that to — I want it to mean something."*

This is, in its way, an act of courage. Not heroism. But the deliberate choice to be present in a moment he is terrified of, and the need to have a witness to that choice.

**Roleplaying hook**: The investigator's response here matters. If they acknowledge what he said without dismissal or false comfort — *"I know"*, or *"Then be there tonight"* — Don Eusebio will stand in that outer ring without flinching when it comes. If they dismiss him or respond with contempt, he will still stand in the ring, but he will be fighting himself as well as the entity's pressure, and Kuyen will feel that in the geometry.

---

## SECOND FRACTURE: QUIRCE'S MOVE

This happens at dusk, as the ritual begins.

### The Setup

Quirce has positioned his theodolite on a rise to the east of the pit — outside the effective geometry range, as he agreed. His notebook is open. He has been, by all appearances, keeping his word.

What he has not kept: the corrected asymmetry in the outer ring. If investigators corrected Kuyen's panel in Session 7, this is no longer a threat. If they did not, it is still present in the geometry and will activate at maximum closure pressure.

But Quirce has a second move that has nothing to do with the panels.

**What Quirce Does**: As the ritual reaches its second phase — when Kuyen deploys the eastern segment and Nahuel anchors the center — Quirce moves. He crosses inside the outer ring with one of his brass instruments. Not aggressively. Quietly. He steps over the chalked outer boundary and crouches at a position that puts him inside the geometry's effective range.

He is not trying to disrupt the ritual. He is trying to observe from the inside. He believes the difference in observational resolution between inside and outside the geometry at peak closure is significant enough to justify the risk. He has — from his perspective — calculated that his single instrument and his body mass will not affect the ritual's success.

**He is wrong**. A person inside the outer ring who is not an anchor, not the opener, and not an aligned Wrong Returned creates a structural inconsistency in the geometry — an unexpected node that the entity will immediately identify and exploit as an unguarded position. It is, mechanically, the equivalent of a gap in the outer ring.

**When investigators notice**: A **Spot Hidden** check when Quirce crosses the boundary. On success: *"He is inside the geometry."* The effective range is clear once you know it — the grass is slightly different inside the ring, the air temperature is one degree lower, the sound from the pit is louder. He crossed a line that, once seen, is obvious.

**The window**: Investigators have one round — perhaps 30 seconds of ritual time — to either pull Quirce out or compensate for his presence before the entity detects the inconsistency. Kuyen will not notice immediately (she is concentrating). Nahuel will know in seconds — and will say nothing, because disrupting his focus to speak would be worse.

---

## SCENE 1 — THE RITUAL BEGINS

### The Assembly

Kuyen leads everyone to their positions. The leather panels are laid in the pit's upper ring — the complete binding geometry in sections that interlock. As each piece is placed, the glow in the blood arcs below intensifies slightly, as if the new geometry is resonating with the existing pattern.

**Positions**:
- **Nahuel**: Center of the symbol. Kneeling. Arms at his sides. Eyes open.
- **Kuyen**: Mobile, moving between sections, maintaining the symbol, the working in her hands and voice.
- **Héctor and Marta**: Eastern and western anchor positions in the outer ring. They stand without instruction, as if they've been to a rehearsal the investigators weren't invited to.
- **Silvio**: Northern position. He stands with his back to the ritual, facing outward — he is not maintaining the symbol, he is listening for the entity's movements in the geometry and will call direction if it approaches.
- **Don Eusebio**: Southern outer ring position — closest to the pit's edge on the side where the opening is strongest. Kuyen has placed him there deliberately. He knows it.
- **Investigators**: Fill remaining positions as needed. If there are not enough investigators to complete the outer ring, Kuyen has prepared for this: she walks two positions at once, but her working suffers.

**Read aloud — the opening**:

> *The sun touches the horizon. Kuyen's voice begins — not loud, not melodic, the same angular chant from the creek bed weeks ago. She moves between the panels, and as she passes each section it brightens. The entity in the pit responds immediately: the vibration in the chest deepens. The carved walls below pulse with a light that is not warm.*
>
> *Nahuel closes his eyes and opens them and is somewhere else. Not gone — present, but the presence is directed inward, into the center of the shape. His breathing slows to something close to absence.*
>
> *The geometry above the pit begins to close. You can feel it — not with the body's normal senses, but with something underneath them. Something that knows angles even when it can't see them.*

**SAN Check**: 0/1D4 (the ritual beginning — the reality of it, the geometry responding)

---

## SCENE 2 — THE ENTITY RESPONDS

The entity does not wait politely.

Within three minutes of the ritual's opening, the vibration intensifies. The carved walls of the pit begin to produce something other than light: a slow movement of geometry, the lines extending upward along the walls as if being drawn by something below. The entity is countering — not disrupting the geometry, but adding to it, elaborating it, trying to convert the binding symbol into an opening symbol by extension.

Kuyen sees this. She begins counter-drawing faster.

**This creates the first major pressure point**: The geometry is being contested. It is not enough to have the right symbol — the right symbol must be held against an entity that understands it better than any human and can modify it from within.

**Mechanical effect**: At the end of each ritual phase (there are five — see Session 9), the entity makes a contest against the ritual. How well investigators have managed everything to this point determines the difficulty of that contest. If the outer ring is intact, if Nahuel has support, if the asymmetry was corrected, if Quirce is not inside the ring — the contests are manageable. If any of these conditions failed, add one level of difficulty to each contest.

---

## SCENE 3 — THE QUIRCE CRISIS

The moment Quirce crosses the outer ring boundary, several things happen:

1. Silvio turns around. He is facing inward now instead of outward. He says, very quietly: *"Someone is inside the ring who shouldn't be."*
2. Nahuel's breathing changes. The ochre patterns on his palms begin to blur — not wash away, blur, as if the geometry they represent is being distorted near the inconsistency.
3. The entity stops extending the carved lines and locates the inconsistency in approximately 15 seconds.

**If investigators act immediately** (pull Quirce out or physically replace him with a correct participant):
- The entity's attention is briefly on the gap, then returns to contesting the ritual. One extra difficulty level for this phase only. No permanent damage.

**If investigators cannot act immediately** (they're in positions, they'd have to break their position):
- The entity invades through the Quirce-gap. Not a full breach — more like a finger inserted into a wound. One investigator in a nearby outer ring position takes 1D6 SAN from the proximity. The phase fails and must be restarted, costing 10 minutes of ritual time. The entity is more deeply mapped into the geometry now.

**Quirce's response to being confronted or pulled**:

He does not resist. He steps out when instructed. He is also, if observers are paying attention, shaking — not from cold, from what he experienced inside the geometry for those 15 seconds. His notebook is open and he has been writing, rapidly, in cipher. Whatever he saw from inside the geometry has given him what he came for, and it has cost him something he didn't expect to pay.

He does not speak about what he experienced. Not this session.

---

## SCENE 4 — DON EUSEBIO IN THE RING

The most important scene in this session. Don Eusebio is in the southern outer ring position and the entity knows it. The entity has been aware of Don Eusebio since he first drew the geometry. He is, from the entity's perspective, the most significant node in the outer ring — the one who opened the wound, whose spatial recognition pattern is embedded in the scar's structure.

When the entity locates him in the ring, it shifts its effort. It stops contesting the geometry broadly and begins pressing the southern position specifically.

**What Don Eusebio experiences**:

> *Something is looking at him. Not from the pit — from everywhere at once, from the corners of the space, from the angles the fires make with the flat earth. It is the same attention he felt in the study, in the first months, when things were working and the land was producing and there was a sound from beneath the ground that he told himself was cattle. It is that attention, clarified. Stripped of the comfort of denial.*
>
> *And in that attention, an offer. Not in words. In certainty. The land will produce again. The cattle will be fat. The drought will end. All of it, available, if he simply steps out of the ring. Out of the geometry. Back to the version of things where he is the patrón and the land obeys.*

**Don Eusebio's decision**: This is his final test. He will hear the offer. He will stand there while it is made. Whether he steps out or stays depends on everything that has happened in the last six sessions — specifically whether investigators have treated him as a human being capable of choice, or as a tool to be managed.

**If the relationship has been built (investigators engaged with him honestly)**:

He stays. He grips the outer ring marker — a stone Kuyen placed — with both hands and does not move. His face is not heroic. It is the face of someone experiencing something terrible and not leaving. He says nothing.

**If the relationship was purely transactional or hostile**:

He falters. He takes one step back, one step out of his position. The outer ring geometry breaks. The southern segment of the binding fails. Kuyen shouts. Nahuel reaches his limit. The phase collapses.

**Recovery** (if he steps out): Don Eusebio can be returned to position by an investigator physically escorting him back — hand on his shoulder, clear eye contact, the command: *"Don Eusebio. Back."* He will go. But the position is now defined by his being held there by someone else rather than by his own will, and the geometry will reflect that — it holds, but the resistance to the entity at the southern position is weaker.

**SAN Check** (watching Don Eusebio experience the offer): 0/1D4 — not for the entity, but for the comprehension that this is what he's been running from, and he is not running now.

## END OF SESSION 8 — THE HOLDING POSITION

The session ends not at resolution but at a sustained crisis point. The ritual is running but not complete. One or more of the following situations is live:

1. **The ritual is behind schedule**: The entity has forced at least one phase restart. Time is running out before dawn, after which the geometry's efficacy drops.
2. **Nahuel is at the edge**: He has been the focus for six weeks total now. Tonight's working is the hardest he has faced. If no investigator co-anchored with him, he is at the point where another full phase may not be possible.
3. **The outer ring is intact but fragile**: Don Eusebio held (or was held). Quirce is outside. The Wrong Returned are in position. But everyone is exhausted and the entity has mapped the geometry's weak points.
4. **The phase count**: Whatever phases were completed this session carry forward to Session 9's total of five. If Session 8 completed phases 1–3, Session 9 begins at phase 4. If the entity forced restarts, the count may be lower.

**End image**:

> *The pit below is fully illuminated by its own geometry — a map of itself, glowing from the carved walls, the arcs in the soil, the sections of the leather binding symbol Kuyen has placed. The entity's counter-movement has slowed. Not stopped — but slowed. Something in the outer ring's integrity is holding it back.*
>
> *Kuyen looks around the ring. She counts. She nods — one small, precise motion.*
>
> *"Two more phases. Don't move."*

## GM NOTES

**Don Eusebio's decision is the moral hinge of the campaign**: Everything from Session 1 has been building to him standing in that ring and choosing whether to stay or leave. Make it real. Don't give him an easy out and don't make it impossible. The choice should feel genuinely uncertain.

**Quirce's breach is the final stress test of investigator preparation**: If they caught the asymmetry and corrected it, if they kept Quirce outside the geometry, they have done the work and the consequences are survivable. If they missed things or trusted him too much, the consequences are harder. This is the CoC principle from Arc 1 in its final form: preparation is not guaranteed, but it matters.

**The entity's offer to Don Eusebio should be vivid but wordless**: Describe what he feels, not what he hears. *"The land will produce again"* — but render it as a sensation of abundance, of the ground being fertile beneath his feet, of his life at the estancia restored to what it was before he understood what that cost. The offer is real. He can still take it. That's what makes it an offer.

**Nahuel's status is the mechanical pressure**: Track his condition carefully. If investigators co-anchored in Session 7, he has more reserves. If not, he is at the limit and Session 9 will require a choice about the focus.
