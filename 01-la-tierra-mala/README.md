# ARC 1 — LA TIERRA MALA (Chapters 1–3)

The players begin as hired hunters tasked with pursuing local tribes blamed for cattle deaths and abandoned settlements. Across these three chapters they discover ritual cattle deaths, see La Luz Mala for the first time, mistake a tribal containment ritual for hostile magic, summon a Hound of Tindalos by interrupting it, and realize the tribes were trying to stop **El Patrón**.

## Arc Plot Points

- Players hired to hunt tribes accused of killing cattle.
- Discovery of ritual cattle deaths and corrupted land.
- La Luz Mala reveals ritual geometry.
- Burned chapel and La Llorona reveal failed resistance.
- Tribal ritual is mistaken for hostile magic.
- Players interrupt the ritual.
- Hound of Tindalos manifests.
- Several tribe members die.
- Emergency ritual containment.
- Zombie cattle rise at the end of the act.
- Players realize the tribes were trying to stop El Patrón.

## Key NPCs

- **Don Eusebio Valdez** — desperate landowner, the contract-giver, the actual *opener* (the one who performed the original ritual).
- **Tomás Arredondo** — frightened foreman, partial truth-teller.
- **Concepción** — orphaned servant girl, partially aligned to the geometry.
- **Rosa Quiroga** — survivor of San Ruiz.
- **Dolores Quiroga** — Rosa's sister; died trying to stop it alone (referenced).
- **Kuyen** — tribal leader running the counter-ritual.
- **Nahuel** — unstable ritual focus.
- **El Patrón** — distant manipulator (per `overview.md` framing); seen watching from afar.

## Key Locations

- Estancia La Esperanza (the contract scene)
- The cattle field (south pasture)
- The burial pit
- San Ruiz (abandoned village)
- The burned chapel
- The tribal encampment
