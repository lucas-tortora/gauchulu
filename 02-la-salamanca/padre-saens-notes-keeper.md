## Mechanical levers

**Don Eusebio in outer ring** (p. 61): non-negotiable. His study diagrams (Hard Occult/INT): every variant without opener fails or worsens access.

**Binding section** (pp. 60–80): partial translation still gives eight anchors, four directions, central focus, source-site requirement, peak-pressure warning. Nahuel would have delivered peak-pressure beat in C7 — with Nahuel dead, Marked PCs or entity communication carry it.

**Reader path** (p. 79): PC who reads **aloud at table** → Use ending in C12. Multiple readers: slowest / weightiest wins. Nobody reads aloud → Use closed (Hide/Destroyed only). After seed: that PC hears ritual text verbatim in Saens's voice each chapter.

**Burn manuscript**: C8–C9 improvised; survival = best outcome.

**Never enter study**: leak via vibration, light, pages on wind; Mercedes letter still points to p. 47.

## Bone chamber — keeper context

Saens returned alone the year before he died. Stood in for opener + knowledge-holder + counter-figure. Geometry took him; skull remains at center — **still working**.

- Partial binding held ~6 decades; Antu-Rayo lineage maintained counter-rites until thinned  
- Don Eusebio's ritual widened wound already opening  
- **Do not move skull** — Héctor: *"Sigue trabajando."*  
- Disturbance tiers: see [C5 bone chamber](./chapter-05-el-manuscrito.md#if-they-disturb-the-bones)

**Marta's correction** (spoken, not manuscript): door vs wound — *"No es una herida. Es una puerta. La herida es lo que le hicimos nosotros."*
