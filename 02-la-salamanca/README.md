# ARC 2 — LA SALAMANCA (Chapters 4–6)

Per `overview.md`, this arc takes the players underground into folklore: **El Pombero** as guide, the entrance to **La Salamanca**, the bone halls, and the **Great Wizard of Salamanca**, who is being secretly manipulated by El Patrón. The arc closes with the first explicit reference to the **Necronomicon**.

The detailed prep currently in this folder reflects an **earlier campaign draft** in which Arc 2 is *El Familiar* — the players return to the estancia, confront Don Eusebio, find the Saens manuscript, meet the Wrong Returned, and follow the trail to the Jesuit mission ruins where the original carving was made. Aldao Quirce is introduced as a Buenos Aires "scholar" with a hidden agenda. Use the existing prep as the foundation; the *Expansion Notes* section appended to each chapter bridges that prep to the new Salamanca framing.

## Arc Plot Points (overview.md)

- Players seek answers in folklore and forbidden places.
- El Pombero appears as guide and test.
- Entrance to La Salamanca discovered.
- Failed ritual site outside Salamanca.
- Chudian-style underground labyrinth.
- The Great Wizard of Salamanca revealed.
- El Patrón is secretly manipulating the wizard.
- La Luz Mala and folklore horrors recur inside.
- Discovery that rituals are incomplete without older knowledge.
- First strong reference to the Necronomicon.
