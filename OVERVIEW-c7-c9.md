*A skimmable run-of-show for the restructured Arc-3 closing. Every heading links into the full text in [chapter-07.md](chapter-07.md), [chapter-08.md](chapter-08.md), [chapter-09.md](chapter-09.md). Read this once before the table sits down; run off the chapter files.*

## THE SHAPE OF IT (read this first)

**One afternoon into one night — no eve, no night in between.** The party waits outside the estancia for Quirce to finish folding the tarnished copper into the diagram; the people who must be here arrive; then they ride to the south-field pit and **build the ritual on-site**, and it runs to a **seal before dawn**. The closing procedure is **Padre Saens's own** — the five-stage instructions recovered in the C5 bone chamber ([H-Sa](02-la-salamanca/saens-closing-instructions.md)) — not San Ruiz. Mercedes leads the voice; Marta builds the figure; the Marked feel it and, if they catch it, **carry the base note the dead tribe left behind.**

**Your job all night:** keep the voice going, keep bodies in position, and get the east right (place a body, don't redraw it).

### Assembly, the four things that happen at the pit
1. **Positions assigned aloud** — use the [Positions at a Glance table](chapter-07.md#assembly--marta-directs). Center = Don Eusebio + a Marked PC; east = the still body; west = the Gaucho; north = Silvio; voice = Mercedes + Marta.
2. **[Drawing the closing](chapter-07.md#assembly--marta-directs)** — **Occult** (know the form) + **Art/Craft** (render it true), ~3 rolls. Clean draws = precision; failed = patched panels cost time.
3. **[The goat points to the east](chapter-07.md#the-goat-points-to-the-east)** — she stares a **deal-PC**, then turns her head to the eastern line: the asymmetry discovery, handed over. **East is placed, not redrawn.**
4. **[Marked palms](chapter-07.md#assembly--marta-directs)** — ochre read-points on the anchor's palms (his holding checks −1 step); the deal-PCs' blood-palms owed to the panel line tonight.

**Exit state to write down before C8:** center pair · who holds east · **anchoring note held? (y/n)** · Quirce in or out · Saúl behind the line.

## STANDING MECHANICS — the six levers (glance during play)

- **Anchoring note** ([C7](chapter-07.md#the-anchoring-note-marked-pcs)) → held = reading doesn't float, −1 step all night. Not held = let the silence be felt.
- **The east** ([C7 order](chapter-07.md#the-order-and-the-east) → [C8 incursion](chapter-08.md#the-eastern-incursion)) → **place a body, never redraw.** Blood-palm or Warding braces it. Log rounds held → sets the Phase-5 step.
- **The tarnished copper** ([C7](chapter-07.md#quirce-finishes-the-copper) → [Phase 5](chapter-09.md#phase-five--the-final-seal)) → two-way window. Left in = Phase-5 press comes through it first (Hard regardless) unless dislodged (DES/Throw).
- **Mandinga blood** (deal-PCs) → opened palm to the panel line; usable at the [east tear](chapter-08.md#the-eastern-incursion), owed by the [seal](chapter-09.md#phase-five--the-final-seal). Withheld → wound reopens / gift inverts.
- **The malón** ([C8](chapter-08.md#the-malón-strikes)) → priests spend warriors; the geometry culls them; **players hold**, don't break formation.
- **The goat** → at the rim all night; six pupils + walks **west** at [dawn](chapter-09.md#riding-out).

**The Sanity-10 Marked PC at the center** is the night's thinnest thread — track COR openly; see the note at [Phase Five](chapter-09.md#phase-five--the-final-seal). Death is a player's choice, never the default.

## AFTER THE SEAL (don't rush this — the payload lands here)

- **[The cost](chapter-09.md#the-cost)** — center survives; the Wrong Returned feel it lift; Don Eusebio's **"What do I owe?"** = the campaign's last moral question (don't answer it for them).
- **[Aftermath](chapter-09.md#aftermath)** — Rosa (hostile unless Dolores is named); the land slowly returns; the panels are never burned.
- **[The Coypac relay](chapter-09.md#the-coypac-relay)** — days later: Saúl is dead, Coypac closed **partial**, the book fragment is loose. Saráchaga's name enters. **The dust hasn't lifted → Arc 4.**
- **[Riding out](chapter-09.md#riding-out)** — the goat leaves west; the Gaucho rides west after the priests (rendezvous C11); the party rides east toward Buenos Aires.
