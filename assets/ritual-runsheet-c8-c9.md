## ROLL FOR HOUND — combined rule for the whole night

- **C8 (Phases 1–3)**: two Marked PCs, each their own 2d6. **Cap: 3 fired aftershocks for the whole chapter** — after the third, doubles just produce the pre-roll tilt, nothing more.
- **C9 (Phases 4–5)**: **no Mark rolls at all.** The entity is fully engaged; the phase mechanics *are* the encounter. One optional roll only, in the 10-minute gap before Phase 5 (a Marked PC deliberately mapping the amber geometry — raised threshold).
- **After the seal**: Marks go quiet at this site, permanently. Tell the Marked players their dice can stay in the bag — the silence is itself a beat.

| Source | Effect | Earned |
|---|---|---|

| Human anchor holding the east | east contest **−1 step/phase** while held | tonight |
| Bone-disturbance carry | east **+1 step** (Tier 1: one phase / Tier 2: whole night); Tier 3 also center-CON +1 step | C5 bone chamber |

**Base recalibration — count only these four, each = +1 if failed:**
1. Center anchor unsupported (no second person bracing) — *not failed, a Marked PC is there.*
2. Eastern gap unmitigated (no Warding, no human anchor) — check your lock-in above.
3. Quirce starts inside the ring — *not failed, he starts outside.*
4. Bone-disturbance tier ≥ 2.

**Do not** separately penalize "no trained center" or "asymmetry never corrected" — that cost is already priced into the ritual's base difficulty.

## THE RUNNING ORDER

### 1 · Afternoon — First Fracture
Don Eusebio disappears for an hour, found (if looked for) at the south-pasture fence, considering leaving. **If intercepted**, he admits it plainly and asks to be witnessed. Acknowledge without judgment → he stands without flinching later. Dismiss him → the outer ring loses coherence at his position when it matters.
→ [Full text](../chapter-08.md#first-fracture--don-eusebio-considers-leaving)

### 2 · Dusk — positions taken
- **The Antiquarian's choice: ring or rim?** Ask it now, before the first chant. Into the ring → slots east/co-reader cleanly, no roll. At the rim → one body lighter everywhere, and if the east tears later they cross in *uninvited*, on worse terms (treat like the Quirce gap).
- Palms marked in ochre for Don Eusebio if the Antiquarian rolled Hard+ Occult at creation (eases his Phase 4 CON / Phase 5 POW one step).
- Panels laid, everyone finds their mark.
→ [Full text](../chapter-08.md#the-antiquarian-at-the-ring-added-pc)

### 3 · PHASE 1 — The Opening
Mercedes begins the chant; Marta lays the first panel; the entity responds immediately. **Don Eusebio, before taking the center**, tries the prayer from C1 (*"Por la tierra que fue buena…"*) and can't finish the third line — if the PC who stood with Tomás in C1 finishes it, he nods at them in Phase 3 and holds without flinching all night.
**SAN 0/1D4.** **Roll for Hound** once, at the opening.
→ [Full text](../chapter-08.md#scene-1--the-ritual-begins)

### 4 · THE MALÓN STRIKES — as the first chant goes up
The deal from C6 does not stop this. Priests hold at the rim; warriors charge for the saddlebags and are **culled by the geometry** — run this with the geometry, not player dice. Players' only job: **hold position.**
- Breaching warrior before cull → **+1 phase difficulty (cap +2)**, nearby PC **SAN 0/1**. No restart.
- A PC breaks formation to fight → **phase restarts (−10 min)**, exposed to raider and geometry both.
- Maps recovered inside the ring only after the seal (or lost, your call).
**No Hound roll** — the entity is already on-stage. **SAN 1/1D3** ring-wide.
*Payoff, if caught: the bargained-with warrior may be among the first culled — the priests spent him, deal and all.*
→ [Full text](../chapter-08.md#the-malón-strikes)

### 5 · Between Phase 1 and 2 — Saúl's Return
Saúl gallops in, delivers the Coypac report (**twelve priests, two pits, an ochre man who knows Dadálah's name**) — [H8](handouts-c8-c12.md#h8--saúl-carrascos-spoken-report-c8-between-phases-1-2). **Mechanical payoff: Phase 3 −1 step.** Mercedes tells him to stay behind the circle — he does, for the rest of the night. *(His death comes later, off-screen, after the seal — do not kill him now, do not have him alive in C9.)*
→ [Full text](../chapter-08.md#saúl-carrascos-return)

### 6 · PHASE 2 — The Triple Crisis (run these fast, overlapping)

**a) The Coypac Echo** (mid-phase) — a Marked PC's Regular POW roll delivers the vision: two pits, twelve priests, a figure holding a book-sized object in wax paper — [H9](handouts-c8-c12.md#h9--the-coypac-vision-card-c8-marked-pcs). Redundant: Mercedes confirms it at the bonfire after Phase 5 if missed. **SAN 1/1D4.**

**b) The Eastern Incursion** (scheduled) — the east tears for 1D3 rounds: **CON Hard** per round to hold (Normal with a second hand bracing; automatic Round 1 with a Mandinga Warding). Each failed round: 1D4 SAN to the easterner + 3 min off the clock. **SAN 0/1D3 ring-wide. No 2d6 — scheduled.** *Log rounds held for the Phase 5 modifier.* If the Antiquarian is there: they may spend a round *reading* the wedge instead of bracing (Occult/CM Hard) — real intel, but forfeits that round's CON.
→ [Full text](../chapter-08.md#the-eastern-incursion)

**c) The Quirce Crisis** — Quirce crosses into the ring to observe. **Spot Hidden** notices. One round to pull him or compensate:
- Acted in time → **+1 difficulty this phase only.**
- Nobody could act → **1D6 SAN** to a nearby PC, **phase restarts (−10 min)**, deeper mapping.
*He's negligent, not a saboteur — nobody dies here.*
→ [Full text](../chapter-08.md#scene-3--the-quirce-crisis)

**d) The Cursed Gaucho Breaks West** — in the same 5-second window, El Patrón is visible at the western edge (only the Gaucho, Marked PCs, and Mercedes see him). The Gaucho runs the gap; El Patrón is gone when it closes. **Marta + a Marked PC** cover the western gap — Phase 2 restarts (−10 min). Mercedes, cold: *"Si vuelve a salir del círculo, lo dejamos afuera."* **SAN 0/1D4** for those who saw him.
→ [Full text](../chapter-08.md#the-cursed-gaucho-breaks-formation)

*If the tarnished copper is still in place: this is where its cost lands — Quirce's crossing plays exactly as written, and the entity's outward reach (the malón cull) extends one ring wider. If it was pulled earlier: Quirce's crossing is far less tempting (he already got his reference), and the east runs one step easier this phase.*

### 7 · PHASE 3 — Don Eusebio in the Ring (the chapter's spine)
The entity finds Don Eusebio specifically and makes the **wordless offer** — abundance, fertile land, if he simply steps out. He says nothing here; he answers aloud in Phase 4.
- Relationship built → he grips the center and holds, silent.
- Transactional/hostile → he falters, steps back, the anchor gives, the phase collapses.
- Recovery → the Marked PC beside him (or any PC) holds him: *"Don Eusebio. Back."* He returns, but weaker — held, not holding.
**SAN 0/1D4.**
→ [Full text](../chapter-08.md#scene-4--don-eusebio-in-the-ring)

### 8 · Between phases — Mercedes' hook
Mercedes sits with a quiet Marked PC: *"¿Sentís lo mismo que yo?"* — confirms tonight's binding holds, tomorrow needs something else. Names nothing further (the Necronomicón/vault/Buenos Aires is already known from C6's H7). **No Reader at this table — skip the Reader-recognition addendum entirely; do not retrofit it.** **SAN 0/1.**
→ [Full text](../chapter-08.md#mercedes-between-phases)

### 9 · [End of Chapter 8 — the holding position]
Not a resolution, a pause. Write down: phase count reached, center anchor's condition, east's condition, time remaining before dawn. Mercedes: *"Dos fases más. No se muevan."*
→ [Full text](../chapter-08.md#end-of-chapter-8--the-holding-position)

### 10 · PHASE 4 — The Deep Close (C9)
*If C8 ended before Phase 3, run each missed phase first using this same structure, then continue.*

The entity tests each outer position **in sequence**, ~30 sec each: **CON Normal** (Hard if compromised in C8). **Don Eusebio at the center: CON Hard** (POW if solo; Normal if the Marked PC beside him braces him).

> He shakes once, goes still, says: **_"No."_** One word. This is the *spoken* answer to Phase 3's wordless offer — his actual redemptive moment.

**End of Phase 4**: ~80% sealed, fully amber light. **SAN 0/1D4.**
→ [Full text](../chapter-09.md#phase-four--the-deep-close)

### 11 · Before Phase 5 — the 10-minute check
Mercedes walks the ring, stops at the center, says nothing — asking without words if they can hold the last one. Answer honestly.
→ [Full text](../chapter-09.md#phase-five--the-final-seal)

### 12 · PHASE 5 — The Final Seal (3-round climax)

**Round 1 — the press.** Anchor's **POW Hard** (Normal if a Marked PC identified the approach vector via C7's entity-speech or Mercedes' "put someone you trust on the east"). Co-anchoring here skips Round 2's crisis entirely (cost: **SAN 1/1D6 + CON check** to the co-anchor).

**Round 2 — the offer to the center.** The entity shows the anchor exactly what closing costs *them* — losing the connection vs. keeping it. One round to decide; a co-anchor holding hands answers wordlessly. They hold. Geometry tightens.

**Round 3 — the seal.** Marta draws the last line. The pit seals bottom-up, the sound **stops** (not fades — stops), the extra two meters of carved wall vanish. **The chant**: *"Antu kishu, lan amukey"* — everyone in the ring says it, including PCs (no roll, just say it). The **missing second voice** (*Newen mapu kelluiñ*, Kuyen's, dead with her) stays missing — let the silence land — **unless** the Antiquarian rolled Extreme Occult and chooses to supply it (full efficacy, imperfectly).

**But the dust does not lift.** Mercedes: *"Lo de acá se cerró. Lo de allá no."* — the campaign's open hook into Arc 4.

**SAN 1/1D6** (not horror — the shock of genuine resolution).
→ [Full text](../chapter-09.md#phase-five--the-final-seal)

### 13 · THE COST — immediate aftermath, one NPC at a time
- **Center anchor** (Eusebio + Marked PC): both alive. Months of recovery, nightmares, never comfortable with right angles again.
- **Héctor & Marta**: Marta flinches, laughs — the alignment fades over weeks, not instantly. (If she co-anchored: burns off in one night.)
- **Silvio**: collapses, *"Paró."* Then, to Eusebio: *"Lo hicieron."*
- **Don Eusebio**: *"What do I owe?"* — the campaign's last significant choice, **belongs entirely to the players.** Do not steer.
- **Quirce**: *"Tengo una deuda acá."* Leaves before sunrise with his notebook.
→ [Full text](../chapter-09.md#the-cost)

### 14 · AFTERMATH
- **Rosa** (if visited): hostile by default; naming **Dolores** unlocks *"Ninguna la ayudó"* and the planting-over-the-pit beat.
- **The land**: grass rings fade over months, birds return, the fig tree keeps growing, La Luz Mala never returns.
- **The goat**: at the pit rim at dawn — six pupils, then walks **west**. Gone.
→ [Full text](../chapter-09.md#aftermath)

### 15 · Days later — The Coypac Relay
Not dawn — **2–3 days later**, before riding out. An anonymous relay rider (not Saúl — he's dead) brings [H10](handouts-c8-c12.md#h10--the-coypac-relay-report-c9-dawn), signed **Saráchaga** (first appearance of that name). Coypac partially closed; the cult fled north with a Necronomicón fragment. Mercedes: *"Voy a Buenos Aires."*
→ [Full text](../chapter-09.md#the-coypac-relay)

### 16 · Riding Out
Don Eusebio offers to come to Buenos Aires (table's choice to accept). The Cursed Gaucho rides west alone toward Coypac's priests — next seen in C11, at the tannery. The ride east passes San Ruiz (well empty) and the Jesuit ruins, arriving at Buenos Aires on day four.
→ [Full text](../chapter-09.md#riding-out) · **Next: [Chapter 10 — La Llegada](../chapter-10.md#chapter-10--la-llegada)**

## IF THINGS GO SIDEWAYS

- **A phase restarts more than twice**: you're burning the pre-dawn clock — say so out loud (*"the sky is a shade less black"*) so the players feel the stakes without a literal countdown.
- **A PC dies or goes irrecoverably insane mid-ritual**: don't stop the ritual to handle it narratively — the position they held needs someone in it. See the separate replacement-PC options already discussed (the malón warrior Antileo, the ranch hand Anselmo, or a second Keeper runner arriving with Saúl are all staged to appear *tonight* without breaking the sequence above).
- **Table wants to skip a scheduled beat**: don't — Coypac intel, the eastern incursion, and Don Eusebio's Phase 3/4 arc are all load-bearing for later chapters (C9 cost, C10 Coypac relay, Arc 4 setup). Compress the *telling*, not the *fact*.
