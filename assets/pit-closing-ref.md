## What the pit is

A spatial wound, not a physical hole. The entity moves through **angles** — where surfaces meet, where planes intersect. The opening diagram *maintains* the tear in the geometry. The closing diagram seals it.

**The entity cannot be killed.** It can only be displaced by sealing the geometry.

## The eastern gap

The eastern segment has never been completed. Dolores couldn't reach it. The entity actively maintains this gap from its side — when Kuyen tries to extend the binding east, the entity disrupts it specifically.

**To close the gap geometrically, you need the complete closing symbol. At this table, that path is currently closed.**

| Path | Status |
|---|---|
| **Clay symbol** (under chapel floor) | Intact underground but buried under rubble. Retrievable — but requires a return trip, physical excavation, and getting past Rosa. |
| **Rosa's memory** | Rosa knows the complete symbol from memory. She will not share it. She knows what these investigators did to the chapel. |
| **Rosa's ornament** | She will not give it. Same reason. |
| **Chapel stone fragment** | Not recovered. Chapel is in ruins. |
| **Tomás's stone** (partial, reverse) | In investigators' possession. Partial counter-binding — reduces distortion load on a human anchor but cannot replace the full symbol. |

**The only option available tonight: a human anchor.**

One investigator plants on the eastern line. No roll — pure decision. They hold the gap by physical presence and will. The entity's distortion field washes over them. CON check (Hard if already hurt; Regular if healthy). On failure: 1D6 HP. On success or failure: the line holds. Any investigator who takes damage from the distortion and survives **takes the Mark**.

**The clay excavation remains a future path.** The symbol is still down there. Someone could go back to San Ruiz, clear the rubble, retrieve it, and bring it to the pit for the final closing in C8–C9. Rosa's opposition is the obstacle — she is not going to help, and she may not stand aside. That confrontation is its own scene.

## What the closing requires

*(From the Saens manuscript, confirmed by Kuyen)*

1. **8 anchors** placed at the outer ring's twelve stations
2. **4 directions named** aloud in the correct tradition — Kuyen's lineage carries this
3. **1 central focus** — Nahuel, or a Marked investigator, or a human willing to hold the center
4. **The complete binding symbol** deployed at the pit itself — not a proxy location
5. **The closing must be performed at the source** — partial approaches slow it; only the pit closes it
6. **The entity will press hardest at peak pressure** — an incomplete closing at maximum ritual intensity produces a worse breach than no closing at all. The anchor must refuse the exit the entity offers.

## The Mark and the geometry

A Marked PC reads shape the way other people read a room. They know if a floor plan is on a binding-square or off it. They read ritual objects by angle, not translation.

- Mark = **shape perception only**. Not intent, not text, not lies.
- A Marked PC at the eastern gap knows its size and vector without being told.
- Using the Mark actively in a scene raises the Hound-trigger threshold. See `assets/geometric-perception-cues.md`.
