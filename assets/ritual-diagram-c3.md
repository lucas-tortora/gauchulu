# C3 — RITUAL DIAGRAM
### *El Ritual Roto — The Working at the Creek Bed*
*Print and keep beside the dice. Use with `assets/tribal-binding.svg`.*

---

## THE SPACE

Dry creek bed. Banks 2m high, ~30m across. Natural bowl — no sight lines from outside. The creek amplifies sound inward and blocks it outward. Ten degrees cooler than the surrounding land. The fires do not smoke.

## WHAT PLAYERS NEED TO DO

### Before the ritual begins (~10 min)

The first decision is not about the ritual. It is about **Cata**.

Investigators arrive to find the eastern position already occupied — an unwilling anchor, bound at the wrists, kneeling on the line. Kuyen will not explain this first. She will run the diagnostic conversation (chapel damage, stone fragment, Rosa) while Cata is visible in the background. The players will have to decide whether to raise it, and what to do.

**If a PC volunteers to replace her**: Kuyen unbinds Cata without discussion and moves her to the camp edge. The volunteering PC takes the eastern position. The rest of the pre-ritual conversation proceeds normally.

**If players say nothing and let the ritual begin**: Cata is the anchor. The ritual proceeds. Her outcome is determined at R4.

**If players try to free her against Kuyen's wishes**: Kuyen will block this. *"Si la sueltan ahora se cae todo."* If they force it anyway, Round 1 begins with an empty eastern gap.

---

The second decision, once the anchor question is resolved:

- Explain the chapel to Kuyen. She needs to know the damage.
- Identify the gravely injured PC's HP. Kuyen will exclude them.
- If Cata was freed: decide which PC takes the east. Two eligible investigators. One anchors east. The other supports Nahuel or covers breaches.
- If Cata was not freed: she is the anchor. Players must decide, during the working, whether to intervene.

**This table has no complete symbol. The east cannot be closed geometrically. Someone holds it. That is the only option. The question is who.**

---

### Round 1 — Chaos

**What happens automatically:**
- The working intensifies. The chant becomes a single repeated line on twelve-breath cycles.
- Four tribe members die simultaneously. They do not bleed. They simply stop being organized the way living things are.
- Nahuel collapses sideways. He is not dead — he is overwhelmed.
- The Hound arrives at the eastern gap. It eats the ochre lines at the edge — not destroying them, consuming their certainty.
- Kuyen is already moving, redrawing pigment lines, low fast Mapudungun.
- **Cata attempts to pull the eastern stake free: STR Hard (~25%).** Her watchers are dead or SAN-frozen. Best distraction window, worst physical condition. On fail: stake shifts, clay disturbed — she has moved it. On success: one hand free. Kuyen shouts at the surviving W tribe member: *"¡Aguántala!"* — costs them their position.

**SAN:** 1/1D6 — first manifestation.

**Player options:**

| Action | Roll | Effect |
|---|---|---|
| Anchor Nahuel — grip his hands, give him something to hold | None — pure decision | He stabilizes. Automatic contribution R2. |
| Guard Kuyen physically | STR or CON | Absorb distortion from entity's presence near her |
| Break angles with fire | None — throw a brand toward the Hound | Pushes it back 1m for 1 round |
| Attack the entity | Weapons pass through | No effect except fire |
| Move to the eastern line | None — pure decision | Sets up the anchor for R4 |
| Help Cata | Opposed STR vs tribe member | She's half-free; west arc destabilizes; a PC must cover it |

---

### Round 2 — Testing

**What happens automatically:**
- The Hound does not advance. It probes. It tests the northern arc — a stone marker shifts, the N line fades. Then it tests the southern arc — the puma marker drifts east on its own, S line weakening. Two arcs threatened simultaneously.
- The eastern window deepens: too clear, too open, like looking into a room with wrong dimensions.
- Kuyen cannot cover both breaches. She goes north. The south tribe member holds their position, chanting, but the south line is weakening and proximity carries SAN 0/1.
- **Cata attempts again: STR Regular (~45%).** Kuyen is at the north breach. W tribe member returned to position after R1. No one watching the east. The stake loosened in R1 whether she succeeded or not. On success: she is free and on her feet. She runs — a PC must immediately fill the eastern position or R4 has no anchor.

**Mindblast (Hound spell — optional):** If a PC is about to complete a critical action — covering a breach, reaching Nahuel, moving to the eastern line — the Hound's testing pulse catches them. Force-angle vision: every angle in the diagram resolves simultaneously, too many at once. *POW check or lose next action.* Timed to the moment losing one action costs most.

**Player options:**

| Action | Roll | Effect |
|---|---|---|
| Anchor Nahuel (if established R1) | None | Automatic contribution — his hold is solid this round |
| Cover north breach — hold the marker | DEX; SAN 0/1 from proximity | Restores N line |
| Cover south breach — hold the marker | DEX; SAN 0/1 from proximity | Restores S line |
| Maintain fire pressure on the Hound | None | Delays advance 1 round |
| Move to eastern line (if not yet there) | None | Last reliable chance to be in position for R4 |
| Stop Cata | Opposed STR | She has both hands now and is fighting for her life — moral weight |

---

### Round 3 — Penetration

**What happens automatically:**
- The Hound finds an angle. The southeast diagonal — held by a tribe member who died in R1, never reinforced — gives it a geometric pocket: where the S arc's weakness and the E gap's uncertainty overlap. It moves through that angle. **It is now inside the outer ring.** Between the southeast diagonal and Nahuel.
- The wrongness is no longer external. It is in here with them.
- Kuyen's lines hold the inner ring but the entity is inside the boundary she set.
- **Cata: SAN 0/1D4 first (Hound at close range, ~10m, not focused on her but present).** On SAN fail: she freezes, muscles locked, cannot act. On SAN success: **STR Easy (~65%).** The Hound's passage through the SE angle has radiated ground distortion — the clay at her stakes is briefly uncertain. On success: she is free. She will move to east but does not run yet.

**SAN:** 0/1D6 — entity inside the working.

**THE GOAT WINDOW:**

If a PC made the deal at San Ruiz, the goat acts now. She crosses the ochre lines without sound, without apparent effect on the working, and charges the Hound from the west.

*Fighting (Gore) 85% to connect. On connect: contested POW — her 115 vs. Hound's avg 120.*

| Result | Effect |
|---|---|
| **Goat wins POW** | Hound staggers, angles reverse involuntarily, retreats through SE diagonal to outside the ring. One round bought. SE diagonal snaps back to clarity. Kuyen gets a beat to reinforce it. Goat returns to camp edge — already there before anyone calls out. |
| **Goat loses POW** | She connects but doesn't move it. Takes the distortion load directly. Goes inert at the edge of the inner ring — not dead, but pulled inward and beyond reach for 1D6 hours. She will rise ahead of the party, but she is not here for R4. The deal-PC feels the contract shift in their chest. |
| **No deal made** | The goat does not move. She has been facing the Hound's position precisely since it arrived. She watches. This is not her ritual. The deal was the permission. |

**Dread Curse of Azathoth (Hound spell — optional):** If the goat's intervention pushed the Hound back and one investigator has been the most effective counter this round, the Hound marks them from outside the ring. *POW vs POW (120 vs target).* On Hound success: permanent POW loss 1D6. Not tonight — the investigator wakes tomorrow slightly less than they were. They will not know when it happened.

**Player options:**

| Action | Roll | Effect |
|---|---|---|
| Reinforce SE diagonal — replace the spiral marker | DEX | Closes the breach; prevents second entry through that angle |
| Fire at the Hound while inside | None | Forces it toward outer ring — doesn't expel, gives Kuyen angles to work with |
| Guard inner ring — between Hound and Nahuel | CON Hard | Absorb distortion; you are sharing space with it |
| Move to eastern line — last moment | None | Arriving now means being in position for R4 |
| Help Cata if she breaks free | — | She will run east in R4 regardless |

---

### Round 4 — Climax

**What happens automatically:**
- The Hound flows toward center — through the angles between things. Patient. Not charging. It has found the geometry that leads to Nahuel and it is following it.
- Nahuel cries out. Not pain. Effort.
- Kuyen's hands move faster than they should be able to.
- The eastern gap is fully open.
- **Cata is free.** No roll this round — the distortion across three rounds has made the clay around her stakes uncertain. They tilt outward. The ropes go slack. She stands up. She looks east. She runs.

**When she crosses the ochre arc, her running body creates the angle.** Her lean at sprint — fifteen to twenty degrees — across the termination point of the line. The mist appears at her footprints, not at the gap. Dense, localized, wrong temperature. She is already four strides east, running toward the eastern bank, running toward the Hound's entry point without knowing it.

**Kuyen shouts — first time she has raised her voice:**
> *"¡El este! ¡Que alguien sostenga el este!"*

**Decision point — no skill check. Pure decision:**

| Choice | Consequence |
|---|---|
| **Investigator plants on the broken line** | CON check: Hard if they've taken damage, Regular if clean. Fail: 1D6 HP. Pass or fail: the line holds. Kuyen completes the symbol. Hound folds out. **Damage taken = the Mark.** |
| **No one holds the east** | Kuyen covers it herself. 1D6 HP minimum. She completes the working. Something in how she holds herself afterward is permanently different. She sits on the ground and does not speak for a long time. No Marks issued. |
| **Goat still active, PC goes down mid-step** | She cannot hold the anchor — not human, cannot close the geometry. But she absorbs part of the distortion load: reduces the CON check from Hard to Regular for a damaged PC. She cannot substitute for the anchor. |
| **Ritual fully collapses** (interference, or Kuyen goes down) | The Hound passes through. Permanent focal point. Four more deaths before dawn. Worse position entering Arc 2. |

## AFTER THE RITUAL

**If the east was held — what Kuyen says:**

*(After the silence, when she speaks:)*

> *"La Dolores aguantó catorce meses. Ustedes tardaron una tarde."*
>
> *(Beat.)*
>
> *"El ritual estaba casi completo. Ahora empezamos el cierre final desde acá. Un trabajo más, en el pozo. Si quieren ser útiles: métannos al pozo. El Valdez no nos va a dejar acercarnos."*

Then, to whoever held the east:

> *"¿Saben lo que hay en el pozo? ¿Lo vieron?"*

She is not testing. She wants to know what she is working with.
