# ARC 4 — BUENOS AIRES & THE HEIST (Chapters 10–12)

The frontier closes. The campaign moves into Buenos Aires and the **University of Buenos Aires**, the secret containment site of the **Necronomicon**. The Keepers are real and divided. **El Patrón** has been waiting for the players to arrive. The arc ends with the central question of the campaign: *who should possess the worst book in the world?*

This arc has no prior detailed prep — the three chapters in this folder were written from `overview.md` for this restructure. They follow the style and depth of the Arc 1–3 detailed chapters: opening situation, scene-by-scene structure, NPC portraits with voice and tells, read-aloud passages, mechanical hooks, and clue ledgers carried forward.

## Arc Plot Points

- Arrival in Buenos Aires after frontier horrors.
- University of Buenos Aires revealed as containment site.
- Secret keeper group protects the Necronomicon.
- Debate with Doña Mercedes over whether to move the book.
- El Patrón escalates rituals to force the decision.
- Failed containment proves ordinary means are not enough.
- Decision made to transport the Necronomicon.
- El Patrón's true plan revealed: he wanted the transport all along.
- Necronomicon transport heist encounter.
- Hound marks and city geometry make the city dangerous.
- Final confrontation: El Patrón, the book, and who controls the future.

## Key NPCs

- **Doña Mercedes** — Keeper, moral center, the campaign's named teacher.
- **Don Eladio Saráchaga** — senior Keeper, dissenter; argues against moving the book.
- **Padre Ramón Albarrán** — Keeper-priest, archive's custodian.
- **Captain Galíndez** — retired soldier, the Keepers' practical hand.
- **Ana Bermúdez** — junior Keeper / cipher; secretly Quirce's correspondent (turned).
- **Aldao Quirce** — returns as compromised Keeper-adjacent scholar.
- **El Patrón** — *avatar of Nyarlathotep*; courteous, present, named.
- **The Cursed Gaucho** — vengeance ally; promises to die at the dockyards.
- **Surviving tribal allies** — Kuyen (if alive), Nahuel, others — present for the final binding.
- **Surviving Wrong Returned** — Héctor, Marta — geometric anchors of last resort.

## Key Locations

- The University of Buenos Aires (Manzana de las Luces, recently founded 1821)
- The hidden vault beneath the university
- The Keeper meeting house (a private home in the Barrio del Alto)
- Buenos Aires streets during the transport
- The dockyards and the *Riachuelo* — the final ritual site
