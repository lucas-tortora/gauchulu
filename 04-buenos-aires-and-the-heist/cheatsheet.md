* **Weak Point 3 — the dock**: El Patrón arrives walking, bleeding, smiling: *"Gracias por traérmelo… Ustedes me lo dibujaron esta noche, calle por calle."* **The geometry locks.** SAN 1/1D8.

Galíndez frozen (*"Quieto."* — SAN 1D6, out for the night). El Patrón names Mercedes' copy aloud (the inoculation pays). Mercedes: *"Decidan."* Sixty seconds.

**ACT 3 — THE CHOICE** (no Keeper steering; **no Mark rolls**; options on paper)

* **USE** *(gate: C5 seed + C8 recognition + C11 cloister, ALL logged — otherwise not on the table; do not retrofit)*

  * Hand the player the **physical Latin card** ([H15](../assets/handouts-c8-c12.md#h15--the-readers-card-c12-use-ending--print-before-the-session)): reading it aloud at the table IS the binding

  * Reader hesitates → Mercedes' fifteen-second silence + *"…mañana lo hago yo, mal, y no funciona."* Refusal has the written bad ending; be willing to run it.

* **DESTROYED** *(always available — the safety valve; Mercedes can rewrite the parchment from memory in 15 min)*
  * Circle forms; **the cost is named before it is paid** — Mercedes will not read until the Marked decide among themselves. Real 5–10 minutes; no facilitation.
  * ⚠️ **[THIS TABLE] 2 Marked PCs → Anchor (dies) / Pillar (POW + one sense)**. **Keeper valve, decide in advance (P19)**: *Marta may volunteer as Anchor* — the only living aligned person; offered **only if the table earned the Wrong Returned's trust**; both PCs then shift down a tier (Pillar/Wall) and Héctor's epilogue is rewritten in silence. Either version is honest.
  * The cuchillo on the chest (or a Marked PC's own blood — that PC takes one tier higher) · Mercedes reads · **the third line is unspoken** — Kuyen's chant missing, a felt silence · the ink rises as smoke · El Patrón becomes *less*, then **gone** — not bound, gone
  * Mercedes survives; hair white and gone by morning, back in a year; Keeper of no book

* **BAD ENDINGS**: deal accepted in C11 · all flee the dock · Reader refuses and Mercedes fails at dawn
