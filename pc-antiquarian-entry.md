## THE STANCE (ideology → table role)

**There is something beyond the reach of human science, and the geometry is the way to it.** Not a thing to be served, not a thing to be feared — a thing to be _understood_. The Antiquarian makes no moral claim about it: whether the geometry is good or evil is, to them, a category error, like asking whether gravity is good. They hold only that it is the most important knowable thing there is, and that comprehension is worth nearly any cost. That drive — not allegiance, not reverence — is how they play:

- **A third pole, not Quirce's opposite.** [Quirce](/chat/chapters-05-12-combined.md#quirce-arrives) is also here for understanding — but Quirce documents for the network's purposes and lets nothing change him. The Antiquarian wants to understand _for its own sake_ and is willing to be changed by it. They share Quirce's amorality and reject his shallowness: to the Antiquarian, measuring a thing from the outside while refusing to let it touch you is a failure of nerve, not rigor. Expect **kinship and friction in the same breath** — and note that both of them actually want the closing to _proceed_, for opposite reasons. (This is subtler and better than a simple rival.)
- **A seeker, not a victim and not a zealot.** Unlike the Wrong Returned (broken by the geometry) or Don Eusebio (ruined by it), the Antiquarian _chose_ to come toward the wound — calmly, on purpose, to watch it operate. Not glad in a warm way, not reverent in a holy way: _rapt_. Play that as quietly unsettling. **Player-controlled — leave the line between curiosity and recklessness for the player to walk.**
- **Not invested in the outcome.** The live wire. By default the Antiquarian does not care whether the pit closes — they want to be _inside_ the working because participation is the deepest form of understanding, but a _failed_ closing is also data. Whether they hold the line when it breaks or let it ride to see what happens is a real player choice, not a foregone one. Don't resolve it for them.
- **Loosely in contact with the secret society — never initiated.** Their connection runs through their mentor's circle (below), as fringe correspondence under a half-name. They arrive knowing fragments and theories but **not the spine** (no Necronomicón, no vault, no El Patrón by name) — they learn it alongside the table.

## BACKSTORY & THE LOOSE TIE — Leipzig, and why they arrive now

**Leipzig.** The Antiquarian trained under a mentor in **Leipzig** who studied geometries like these — the European, post-Enlightenment end of the same knowledge. Not folk superstition but a _learned_ tradition: the Naturphilosophie milieu, hermetic mathematics, the book-fair underworld of forbidden printings — scholars who treated certain forms as apertures onto the noumenal, onto what lies past what science can reach. That lineage is **also the root of the secret society at the edge of this campaign**: the "European-trained occultists" whose epistolary signature [_sub signo magno_](/chat/chapters-05-12-combined.md#quirces-announcement-letter) surfaces in Quirce's letters. The mentor's circle and Quirce's network draw from the same European source. That is the "loose contact" — the Antiquarian inherited fringe correspondence _through Leipzig_, was never inducted, and is Network-rooted without being Network-loyal.

**The mentor (player's to finalize — options):** _dead_, having reached a limit studying one of these geometries and stopped there — the Antiquarian intends to go _past_ where the mentor stopped (a quiet echo of [Saens](/chat/chapters-05-12-combined.md#the-bone-chamber), who also could not finish). Or _vanished_ mid-study, no body. Or simply _old and still in Leipzig_, the Antiquarian their field-traveler in the New World. Lean toward the first if you want the character to carry a private intellectual debt — the mentor as the limit they came to surpass.

**Why now.** Word of a _genuine_ closing at La Esperanza leaked through the same network correspondence that reaches Quirce. A real working of the kind they have only read about, run by people who survived the wound — the Antiquarian crossed an ocean and a continent **to witness it from inside.** Not to help. Not to stop it. To understand the thing operating.

**Hook item (optional, strong):** a single vellum leaf bought years ago — a fragment of the [dual diagram](/chat/chapters-05-12-combined.md#the-jesuit-mission-ruins) with marginal Latin in an old neat hand — the object that first proved to them these geometries were real and pointed their scholarship here. Played out, it detonates in Buenos Aires: the leaf descends from [Mercedes' 1781 copy](/chat/chapters-05-12-combined.md#do%C3%B1a-mercedes-alone), which escaped through the Lacasa archive into the European trade the Antiquarian later bought from. When Mercedes sees it, she goes still. _(Hand the player the leaf; let them not know what it is for a long time.)_

**The justification:** a seeker feels the closing from miles off. Like the Wrong Returned who "felt it from Tres Lagunas," the Antiquarian sensed the geometry waking — the **Leipzig** training is what let them read the signs at a distance — and rode the last stretch hard to reach it.

**Where it goes:** end of [Dusk — departure](/chat/chapters-05-12-combined.md#dusk--departure), just before [Scene 1 — the ritual begins](/chat/chapters-05-12-combined.md#scene-1--the-ritual-begins). The Antiquarian rides in on the last of it. _(Gentlest alternative, no pressure: a calm dawn arrival in the **C8 day phase** — splice at the top of [The day before the night](/chat/chapters-05-12-combined.md#the-day-before-the-night).)_

> _The horse is finished — foam at the bit, head low — and the rider half-falls out of the saddle and does not care. They have been riding since they felt it, a day and a night, toward a place they only half-knew the name of. They take in the camp in one sweep — the panels, the carved arcs going faintly amber in the dusk, the people finding their marks — and they do not ask what is happening. They already know what is happening. They ask the only thing that matters to them: "¿Hay un lugar para mí en el círculo, o llegué tarde?"_
> 
> **[ES]** _El caballo está reventado — espuma en el freno, la cabeza baja — y el jinete se baja a medias de la montura y no le importa. Viene cabalgando desde que lo sintió, un día y una noche, hacia un lugar cuyo nombre apenas conocía. Abarca el campamento de una mirada — los paneles, los arcos tallados poniéndose levemente ámbar en el crepúsculo, la gente buscando su sitio — y no pregunta qué está pasando. Ya sabe qué está pasando. Pregunta lo único que le importa: "¿Hay un lugar para mí en el círculo, o llegué tarde?"_
> 
> **[EN]** _"Is there a place for me in the circle, or am I too late?"_

**The decision is the scene.** A stranger is asking to be let into a closing that starts in minutes. **Mercedes makes the call** — and she will look to the players, because they are the ones who have to stand beside whoever she admits: _"¿Lo conocen? Porque en cinco minutos no hay tiempo de conocer a nadie."_ **Quirce, if present**, supplies the recommendation that is also a warning — a thin smile of recognition: _"Lo conozco de nombre. Estudió en Leipzig, con gente que sabe de esto. No viene a detenerlo ni a empezarlo — viene a ver adentro. Como yo. No va a romper nada a propósito."_ _(A beat.)_ _"Pero no le va a importar si se rompe. Ténganlo presente."_ — _"I know them by name. They studied in Leipzig, with people who know this work. They didn't come to stop it or start it — they came to see inside it. Like me. They won't break anything on purpose."_ _(A beat.)_ _"But they won't care if it breaks. Keep that in mind."_

- **Let in** → slot them at the [eastern line or as co-reader](/chat/chapters-05-12-combined.md#role-in-the-closing) before Phase 1. Clean, full benefit: the ritual gains a literate second hand exactly where the table was thin. No Hound roll — the geometry is not yet active.
- **Kept out** → they stand at the rim and do not leave. The ring is one body lighter and everyone feels it. If the [east tears](/chat/chapters-05-12-combined.md#the-eastern-incursion) later, they will **cross the line uninvited** to reach it — which converts this into the mid-ritual fallback below, on worse terms, with no one having chosen it.

**SAN Check** at the rim: 0/1 — the vertigo of a lifelong belief turning out to be literally true.

### Fallback — mid-ritual entry (only if they're refused at the rim)

**Where it goes:** inside [Scene 2 — the entity responds](/chat/chapters-05-12-combined.md#scene-2--the-entity-responds) or at the moment of [the Eastern Incursion](/chat/chapters-05-12-combined.md#the-eastern-incursion). The ritual is _running_; the geometry is active and contested; the entity is pressing the east. And a rider comes out of the dark, straight at the field, not slowing.

> _No one hears the horse over the chant until it is almost on them — and then it is too close, a dark shape at a gallop coming down the south slope toward the laid panels, and the rider is not steering for the fires or the people. They are steering for the gap. For the eastern seam, the thin place, the exact point where the held note is going to break — as if they can see it, as if it is the only lit thing on the whole dark plain. They come off the horse at a run. They do not ask permission. They put their hands on the failing line._
> 
> **[ES]** _Nadie oye el caballo por encima del canto hasta que ya está casi encima — y entonces está demasiado cerca, una silueta oscura al galope bajando por la ladera sur hacia los paneles tendidos, y el jinete no apunta a los fuegos ni a la gente. Apunta al hueco. A la costura del este, al lugar fino, al punto exacto donde la nota tensa está por romperse — como si lo viera, como si fuera lo único iluminado en toda la llanura oscura. Se baja del caballo a la carrera. No pide permiso. Pone las manos sobre la línea que cede._

**The table has one round to decide what this is** — because the entity already has. An unbriefed stranger entering the active field is, structurally, a new node, exactly like [Quirce crossing the ring](/chat/chapters-05-12-combined.md#scene-3--the-quirce-crisis) — _unless_ they are guided to a _correct_ position before the entity locates them (~15 seconds). The difference from Quirce: **the Antiquarian is reaching for the break, not for a view.** Which way it resolves is the players' call under pressure:

- **Guided / accepted (boon).** If a Marked PC calls them onto the eastern line, or Mercedes/Marta accepts their hands on it within the round, they become **last-second reinforcement at the exact moment of [the Eastern Incursion](/chat/chapters-05-12-combined.md#the-eastern-incursion)** — count them as the second person bracing the easterner (the eastern hold drops from **Hard to Normal**), or as filling an empty eastern slot outright. The believer arrives precisely when the thing they believe in is breaking, and holds it. **It works because they were right about where the wound was.**
- **Unplaced (crisis).** If no one gets them to a correct position in the window, the entity reads the new node: the phase **destabilizes and restarts** (−10 min, as with the Quirce gap), and a nearby PC takes **1D6 SAN** from the proximity. They are not a saboteur — they are a stranger who acted on faith in the dark and became a hole. Recoverable next phase if then placed.

**Quirce, if present**, is the one who _names_ what just happened — and his line tells the players which way to jump: _"¡Ese no es un atacante — es un nodo! ¡Pónganlo en una posición o sáquenlo, ya!"_ — _"That's not an attacker — it's a node! Put them in a position or pull them out, now!"_

> **This is the natural Marking moment.** Hands on active, contested geometry with the entity pressing through it — the entity reads them. **They are Marked here** (SAN 1/1D4 on top of any above). For this PC that is not a wound; it is the proof they crossed a country to get. Let them feel it land as confirmation, not horror — and from C9 onward they carry the Mark east like the [two who already have it](/chat/chapters-05-12-combined.md#chapter-8--la-traici%C3%B3n).

**SAN Check**: 1/1D4 (the entry into live geometry) + the Marking note above.

## ROLE IN THE CLOSING

Drop them straight into the [restructured C8 positions](/chat/chapters-05-12-combined.md#opening-situation). They fill the table's two real gaps:

1. **Co-reader with Mercedes.** A second literate ritual hand — they can hold the procedure from Saens's manuscript while Mercedes leads, steadying tempo. Mechanically this is the _"center anchor supported / director not walking two roles"_ relief the improvised ritual badly needs.
2. **Eastern line (recommended).** Their belief makes them willing to stand on the [thin side](/chat/chapters-05-12-combined.md#the-eastern-incursion) — and as a literate occultist they can _try to read_ the asymmetry rather than merely brace it. **This directly mitigates P13 condition (2)** (eastern gap held by a human anchor), making each phase contest one step easier on the east. If they attempt to _correct_ the line, they hit the same wall Marta does — _"I can see it's wrong; I cannot draw the one that belongs"_ — and learn, viscerally, the limit of scholarship against the real thing. Good for the ideology.

> **The Quirce friction, mechanized.** If Quirce is still angling to keep his eastern observation-window open, the Antiquarian is the PC most likely to _notice_ and _contest_ it — give them the [Hard Occult catch](/chat/chapters-05-12-combined.md#quirces-move) automatically (one believer reads another's hand). Whether they expose Quirce, fight him for the panel, or quietly fix what they can is a player choice with real C8 stakes.

**Becoming Marked (optional):** they arrive _unmarked_ (the [Mark](/chat/chapters-05-12-combined.md#chapter-8--la-traici%C3%B3n) came from C3, which they missed) — which is useful: a literate hand who can _look hard_ at geometry without rolling for the Hound. But if they **co-anchor the center** or take the entity's direct contact in [C8 Scene 4 / C9](/chat/chapters-05-12-combined.md#scene-4--don-eusebio-in-the-ring), that exposure can Mark them on the spot — and for this PC, being Marked is not a curse but the thing they _wanted_: proof, written into the body. Let them choose it with eyes open; price it like the C9 co-anchor cost (SAN 1/1D6 + CON check).

## RELATIONSHIPS (quick)

- **Quirce** — same toolkit, same amorality, _different end._ Quirce documents for the network and stays untouched; the Antiquarian seeks understanding for its own sake and will be changed by it. Kinship and contempt at once — and a shared, unspoken wish for the closing to _proceed_. Not a simple rival; a fellow traveler the Antiquarian finds shallow and Quirce finds dangerously unmoored.
- **Mercedes** — a living echo of _young_ Mercedes: the seeker who wanted to understand and copied a page she shouldn't have, at 24. She distrusts the Antiquarian for exactly that reason — and is the one person at the table who truly understands the drive. Reluctant mentor; she tests them hard before trusting them. The [hook-item leaf](/chat/chapters-05-12-combined.md#do%C3%B1a-mercedes-alone) detonates between them in Buenos Aires.
- **Don Eusebio** — the Antiquarian sees the wound as a _phenomenon_, not a crime, so they speak to Eusebio without the contempt everyone else carries — which can steady his [southern-ring](/chat/chapters-05-12-combined.md#scene-4--don-eusebio-in-the-ring) resolve. (It is not kindness; it is that guilt doesn't interest them. Eusebio may not notice the difference, and it helps either way.)
- **The Wrong Returned** — pure fascination: living evidence of the beyond-science. **The trap for this character:** if the player treats Marta/Héctor as _specimens_ rather than people, that is the exact failure that closes [Marta's C12 valve](/chat/chapters-05-12-combined.md#option-3--destroyed). The seeker's defining temptation, with a real mechanical cost.

Expect this PC to _argue for Use_ and to be genuinely anguished by Destroy — which puts them in direct tension with anyone at the table who wants the book gone. The campaign's central question, pointed at the one character built to want the wrong answer.
